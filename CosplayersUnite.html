Tips For Cosplayers

  • Buy a good wig. Cheap quality wigs may look fake and be of a bad quality. Don't be afraid of looking at more expensive ones.
  • When buying or making a cosplay, make sure it will fit you. If the costume fits you better, you will look better.
  • Add all necessary details. If a character is known for wearing a key around their neck, remember to include it.
