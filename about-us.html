About Us

Cosplayers Unite is a website that allows cosplayers to connect with each other. Want to know if there is anyone around willing to be a part of your cosplay group? You've come to the right place!

Not only is this website good for meeting people, you get to see and share cosplay photos. If you think your Light Yagami outfit is cool and you want to show others, feel free to upload it to the gallery.

Other Services We Offer

Want to learn about upcoming events? Where might you be able to see that cosplay group you like on Youtube perform a skit or participate in a panel? Here on Cosplayers Unite, we inform our viewers of upcoming cosplay events and conventions you may be interested in. As we are an Irish based website, we may miss an event or two in your area so don't be afraid to let us know!
